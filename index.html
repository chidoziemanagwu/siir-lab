Welcome to The UA CII-Lab!​

We leverage the immense capabilities of emerging construction technologies, computational models, and our extensive knowledge and experience in construction engineering and management to solve critical problems at the nexus of construction health and safety, workforce development, sustainable construction, and construction automation.  

The laboratory is under the directorship of Dr. Chuma Nnaji, PhD, MBA. ​
